#  Financial Sales & Profit Dashboard

This project shows a Financial Dashboard that helps understand a companyâ€™s **sales, profit, and product performance** in a clear and simple way.

The dashboard makes it easy to see which products, countries, and customer segments are performing well.

##  About the Dashboard

This dashboard shows:

*  Total Sales
*  Total Profit
*  Total Units Sold
*  Sales by Product
*  Sales by Country
*  Sales by Customer Segment
*  Sales by Month
*  Sales by Discount Band

It also allows filtering by **Year (2013, 2014)** and **Country**.


##  Main Results

* **Total Sales:** 118.73M
* **Total Profit:** 16.89M
* **Units Sold:** 1.13M


##  Key Insights

* The **Government segment** gives the highest sales.
* **Paseo** is the top-selling product.
* Sales are highest in **October**.
* Medium and High discounts bring more sales.
* Sales slowly decrease in some months.
